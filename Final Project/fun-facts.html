Fun Animal Facts

Elephants

Did you know? Elephants are the only mammals that can't jump!

Giraffes

Giraffes have the same number of neck vertebrae as humans - seven!

Penguins

Emperor Penguins can dive up to 1,800 feet deep in the ocean!

Lions

Lions can live up to 10 to 14 years, and it's roar can be heard from 8 km away.

Monkeys

Monkeys can understand written numbers and even count. Some can grasp basic arithmetic concepts and, in rare cases, even multiplication!

Dolphins

Dolphins can recognize themselves in the mirror, and they love to admire themselves.

Kangaroos

Kangaroos are most active during dawn and dusk.

Tigers

A tiger's back legs are longer than its front legs, which makes it a running, jumping, and pouncing machine.

Pandas

A panda year is equivalent to three human years.
