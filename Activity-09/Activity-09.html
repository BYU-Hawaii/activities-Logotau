About Herons

Heron birds are long-legged freshwater and coastal birds in the family Ardeidae, known for their graceful appearance and hunting skills. They typically inhabit wetland environments, where they feed on fish, amphibians, and other small aquatic creatures.

Types of Herons

Explore the various species of herons, including the Great Blue Heron, the Black-crowned Night Heron, and the Little Egret. Each species has its own unique characteristics and habitat preferences.
